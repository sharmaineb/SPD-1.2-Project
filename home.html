Lens

"Lens is an application that can identify Films and TV Shows based on a short form. Say there is a movie from your childhood that you can't remember but you remember small details of the plot and a song that was in the film. When you enter what you remember into the form, films that match your description should pop up and hopefully the film you were looking for is one of them. Another way to use this application, is if you're looking for a recommendation. You can fill out the form by inputting whether you're feeling a film or a tv show, what genre you are currently interested in, and story you're looking for."
